🎮 Flippio

Welcome to the official support page for Flippio, a colorful memory matching game for iOS. Train your memory, beat your best score, and enjoy endless fun.

✨ About the App

Flippio is a polished card-matching game featuring:

  • 3 Difficulty Levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard modes for all skill levels
  • Power-Ups: Reveal All, Peek Two, and Shuffle to strategize your gameplay
  • Leaderboard: Track your best scores and fastest times
  • Haptic & Sound: Customizable feedback for immersive experience

🔒 Privacy Policy

📋 Data Collection

Flippio is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users.

💾 Local Storage Only

All game data—including your scores, best moves, difficulty preferences, and leaderboard statistics—is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never sent to external servers or third parties.

🚫 No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or behavioral monitoring. Your gameplay activity remains completely private.

🎯 No Ads or Third-Party Services

Flippio contains no advertisements, in-app purchases, or integrations with external services.
